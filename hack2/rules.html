Rulebook

Objective

Score the most points by maneuvering your secret Tikis into the top three positions on the board by the end of the round!

Gameplay

On your turn, you must play exactly one action card from your hand. First, select the Tiki on the board you wish to target, then click the card to play it.

Tiki Up (1, 2, or 3)

Move a Tiki up the stack by the number of spaces indicated. It pushes other Tikis down.

Tiki Topple

Move any Tiki from anywhere in the stack straight to the very bottom position.

Tiki Toast

Remove the Tiki at the very bottom of the stack entirely from the game!

End of Round & Scoring

A round ends immediately when only 3 Tikis remain on the board, or everyone runs out of cards.

  • If your 1st Secret Tiki is in 1st Place = 9 Points
  • If your 2nd Secret Tiki is in 2nd Place = 5 Points
  • If your 3rd Secret Tiki is in 3rd Place = 2 Points
